# Spring Arrivals: A Season of New Beginnings

Spring has always felt like a time of renewal. After the cold quiet of winter, the world begins to wake up again.

## Observations

- Days are getting longer
- First flowers are starting to bloom
- There's more energy in the air

## Reflections

This time of year naturally invites reflection on what we want to grow in our lives. Just as plants push through soil toward the sun, we too can push through challenges toward our goals.

> "No winter lasts forever; no spring skips its turn." — Hal Borland

## Simple Joys

- Morning coffee with sunlight streaming through windows
- Evening walks as the air warms up
- Planning summer adventures

Spring reminds us that change is inevitable, but that change can bring beautiful things.
